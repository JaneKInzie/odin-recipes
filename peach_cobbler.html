Peach Cobbler

This quick and easy peach cobbler recipe is a delight. I learned this recipe while working at a North Carolina style barbeque restaurant where it was a staple dessert item. It is one of my family's favorite desserts, especially when topped with vanilla ice cream.

Ingredients
  • Two 29 ounce cans sliced peaches in syrup
  • One cup of milk
  • One heaping cup self-rising flour
  • One heaping cup of sugar
  • One stick of butter
  • Vanilla ice cream (optional)
Steps
  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit.
  2. While oven is preheating, put stick upf butter into casserole dish, and put in oven to melt butter.
  3. Drain peaches well. Check the butter.
  4. In large bowl, add sugar, self-rising flour, and milk. Check the butter.
  5. Whisk together batter. When butter is melted, emove casserole dish from oven.
  6. Pur batter into center of dish. Butter will spread and lay on top of batter.
  7. Starting at edges and working in, add peaches one or two slices at a time. Peaches will push butter toward center.
  8. Baker for one hour or until golden brown.
  9. Let cool slightly, then serve topped with ice cream.
